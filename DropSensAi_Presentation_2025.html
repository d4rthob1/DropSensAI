The Problem

The Opportunity

Introducing DropSensAI

“Meet DropSensAI.”

• Instantly compares viewed prices to current prices.

• Detects drops by SKU and customer.

• Filters by stock levels and thresholds.

• Outputs a clean, ready-to-use target list.

DropSensAI changes that. It analyzes customer browsing data and live price drops, and instantly finds the customers who would be thrilled to know a product they viewed… is now cheaper.

Demo Scenario

It’s simple. DropSensAI takes two data sources: Browsed products and prices. Current product prices and drops. It automatically compares them, filters by stock levels, and pinpoints the customers who deserve to know about a discount.
